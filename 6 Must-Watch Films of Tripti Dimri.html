6 Must-Watch Films of Tripti Dimri

Team FC

1. Mom (2017)

Tripti Dimri's debut in Bollywood began with "Mom" (2017), where she showcased her acting prowess alongside Sridevi, leaving a lasting impact in this intense thriller.

2. Poster Boys (2017)

In the same year, Dimri added a touch of comedy to her repertoire with "Poster Boys," a light-hearted film that demonstrated her versatility as an actress.

3. Laila Majnu (2018)

Dimri took on the challenging role of Laila in this modern adaptation of a classic love story, "Laila Majnu," delivering a compelling performance that resonated with audiences.

4. Bulbbul (2020)

"Bulbbul" marked a significant shift for Dimri as she stepped into the realm of supernatural horror, earning praise for her portrayal of the titular character and contributing to the film's success.

5. Qala (2022)

In Netflix's "Qala," Tripti Dimri captivated audiences with her emotive performance, adding another noteworthy film to her repertoire and showcasing her ability to bring depth to complex characters.

6. Animal (2023)

Dimri's collaboration with Sandeep Reddy Vanga in "Animal" further solidified her position in the industry, contributing to the film's success as it became one of the most anticipated film of the year.
